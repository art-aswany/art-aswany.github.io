üí° Pro Tips for Healthy Boundaries

  • Start small - practice with low-stakes situations first
  • Be consistent - mixed messages weaken boundaries
  • Expect testing - people may push back initially
  • Stay calm and clear - emotions can blur your message
  • Have consequences ready - boundaries without consequences are just suggestions
  • Practice self-care - maintaining boundaries requires energy
