üåü Key Takeaways

Both feeling and thinking are valuable! The goal isn't to change your natural tendency, but to:

  • Understand your default processing style
  • Recognize when the other approach might be more effective
  • Develop flexibility to choose your response consciously
  • Appreciate the value of both perspectives in different situations

"The wise person uses both heart and mind, choosing the right tool for each situation."
