🔋 Understanding Energy Types

Everyone has unique energy patterns influenced by genetics, lifestyle, and personal preferences.

🌅 MORNING LARKS

"Early Birds"

  • Peak energy: 6-10 AM
  • Natural early risers
  • Most productive before noon
  • Prefer early bedtimes
  • Alert and focused mornings
  • Energy drops in evening

🌙 NIGHT OWLS

"Late Bloomers"

  • Peak energy: 6-10 PM
  • Prefer late wake times
  • Most creative at night
  • Slow morning starts
  • Second wind evenings
  • Naturally late sleepers

🌞 MIDDLE BIRDS

"Balanced Types"

  • Peak energy: 10 AM-2 PM
  • Moderate morning energy
  • Steady throughout day
  • Flexible sleep schedule
  • Adaptable to demands
  • Consistent performance

🔄 VARIABLE TYPES

"Shifters"

  • Multiple energy peaks
  • Pattern varies by season
  • Influenced by activities
  • Adaptable rhythms
  • Context-dependent energy
  • Flexible scheduling needs
